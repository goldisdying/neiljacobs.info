About

The story so far

Neil Jacobs is a leading brand and marketing strategist and educator in the Bitcoin industry. With a Big Four CPA background and over 115,000 social media followers, he has driven brand and revenue growth for a wide range of Bitcoin-focused companies, from financial services firms to mining operations.

He co-founded FOMO21.com, a Bitcoin-themed e-commerce brand that has served customers in 67 countries. His Bitcoin journey began over a decade ago, and he has since become a vocal advocate — hosting podcasts, serving on the Board of Directors for a nonprofit advancing Bitcoin, and speaking at major industry conferences.

Neil combines marketing and financial expertise with strategic vision to champion Bitcoin and foster its global adoption.
